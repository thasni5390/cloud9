FOUNDATION AND BASEMENT: Random Rubble masonry PCC if required.

STRUCTURE : Laterite Stone Masonry. 

ROOF SLAB: Reinforced cement concrete.

 FLOORING: Vitrified tiles.

DOORS: Main doors-Wooden, paint finished and wooden frame with Fero and moulded door shutter for others.

WINDOWS: Hard wood framed with MS grills and glass.

 KITCHEN: Rustic tiled flooring, polished granite counter top with glazed-tile to 45cms from counter-top, stainless steel sink with drain board.

PLUMBING: Concealed plumbing with CP fittings, provision for geysers.

SANITARY FITTINGS: Parryware/Cera or equivalent.

PAINTING: Putty finished emulsion paint for interior, anti-fungal weather coat for exteriors. Enamel paint for wood works and MS grills, brand Asian, berger, ICI or equivalent.

Thondayadu-Medical College Jn is an upscale hybrid residential- commercial neighbourhood in Calicut. A calm and serene upmarket residential area just 3km off Thondayad Jn, the epicentre of Calicut city. The area has gradually developed into a prestigious residential colony and is home to many apartment complexes as well. To cater the residential community, prominent shopping areas developed all through the road connecting Thondayad Jn and Medical College. Located within Calicut Corporation City limits, It has various amenities and access specially dedicated to Working Professionals, Elderly citizens, Kids, and youngsters alike, promoting the eco friendly - go green theme in the premise. Area is in demand as a residential hub for its broad roads, clean & green environment and its parks. no wonder it attracts anyone and everyone to the place.
